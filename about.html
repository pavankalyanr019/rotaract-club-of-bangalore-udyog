About Us

Rotaract is a global movement of young leaders under Rotary International that empowers individuals to exchange ideas with leaders in the community, develop leadership and professional skills, and have fun through service. Rotaract clubs bring together people ages 18 and older to exchange ideas with leaders in the community, develop leadership and professional skills, and have fun through service.

Rotaract Club of Bangalore Udyog is part of Rotary International District 3192. We focus on Club Service, Community Service, International Service, and Professional Development. Our members collaborate on flagship and avenue-specific projects that create measurable impact and lasting friendships.

What We Do

  • Plan and execute community service initiatives (health, education, environment).
  • Host fellowships and professional development workshops.
  • Collaborate with Rotaract and Rotary clubs worldwide for international understanding.
  • Lead with integrity, inclusivity, and innovation.
